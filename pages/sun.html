The Sun is a G-type star

The Sun is the star of the Solar System. It is a G-type yellow dwarf and has 8 known planets orbiting around it. The Sun is 4.5 billion years old and is expected to last 5 billion more years as a main sequence star, before it becimes a red giant.

The Sun is vital for life on Earth and has been seen as a deity. Solar calendars are based on the Earth's rotation around the Sun.
